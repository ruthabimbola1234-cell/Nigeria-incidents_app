## Survery Of Causes Of Incidents in Nigeria

### Overview

This project explores incidents reported across Nigeria using structured data to identify trends, patterns, and possible contributing factors. The goal is to support better understanding of incident distribution across regions and time periods through data-driven insights.

Nigeria faces diverse challenges ranging from public safety concerns to socio-economic and environmental pressures. By analyzing incident-related datasets, this project helps highlight key indicators that may influence the occurrence of incidents, such as location, frequency, and demographic or economic context.
